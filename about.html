Years Of Experience

We are delivering high quality
Products

Established in 2000 “VARDHMAN SUPER ABRASIVES” has gained National Recognition as a Quality Producer of Super Abrasive and Stone Polishing Products. The high quality finish and cost-effectiveness of our products have made us the preferred choice of many leading corporates around the world. We are manufacturer of Metal and Resin bonded diamond abrasives for Marble, Granite, Semi Precious & all type of imported onyx and natural stone. As a leading full line manufacturer of Frankfurt, Fickert-140/170, Chemfering, 8” Buff abrasives and Polishing Powder. “VARDHMAN SUPER ABRASIVES” offers precision-built products of high quality and performance for virtually every conceivable manufacturing application.

More about us

Backed by a philosophy of rapid but sustainable change in response to the evolving needs of our customers, we have developed into a mid-sized full range abrasive manufacturer. The company maintains a large manufacturing facility at Sukher near Udaipur, from where VSA products are shipped through out India. The brand has grown in recognition for quality and performance, safety, timely delivery of products, short lead-time, prompt service, competitive prices and reduced grinding cost.
